Truly Awesome Data Mesh People

As more people continue to contribute to the community, we will expand on this page. For now, we want to call out Robert Sahlin for kicking off this process and Zhamak Dehghani for her tireless work in this community.

We also appreciate all those who are out there sharing their stories and insight and will recognize you individually as things progress. Who knows, we might even get some vendors to sponsor some swag.

Lastly, we (I) would like to thank Sam Ramji for helping set me on this data mesh journey and the rest of the team at DataStax for believing this is a worthwhile way to spend salaried time. This community is likely to consume a large portion of my time and I'm glad to be part of an organization that sees the benefit in helping out communities to grow (and live long?) and prosper.

Know anyone else you think should get a shout out, let us know who and why: community@datameshlearning.com.
